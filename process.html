Program Overview

The Google Cloud Arcade Facilitator Program is a comprehensive learning journey that helps you master cloud skills through hands-on labs, earn official certifications, and receive exclusive Google swag.

This program is open to students (B.Tech, BCA, BSc), professionals, and cloud enthusiasts. Follow the steps below to participate and unlock your cloud potential!

1

Join the WhatsApp Group

Join facilitator's WhatsApp group to stay updated with official announcements, resources, and support throughout the program.

Group link: Join WhatsApp Group

You'll receive important updates, deadline reminders, and problem-solving support in this group.

2

Complete the Enrollment Form

The official registration form will go live on 4th August2025 at 5 PM IST. Follow these guidelines to ensure successful registration:

  • Fill in the form carefully with your correct details
  • Use your official name and email ID (Gmail preferred)
  • Enter your Google Cloud Skills Boost public profile link
  • Enter the correct Referral Code: GCAF25C2-IN-7A7-VQS (or as shared by your facilitator)

Incorrect or duplicate entries will not be accepted. Please fill the form carefully to avoid rejection.

Helpful Resources:

How to make your profile public
3

Wait for Enrollment Confirmation

Once the form is submitted correctly, you'll get an enrollment email within 24–48 hours.

This email contains your Access Code to activate your free 2-month subscription to Google Cloud Skills Boost.

If you don't receive the email within 48 hours, check your spam folder before contacting your facilitator.

4

Activate Your FREE 2-Month Subscription

Follow these exact steps to activate your free subscription and get 600 credits:

  1. Open your browser in Incognito Mode (Ctrl+Shift+N)
  2. Go to: https://cloudskillsboost.google/catalog?keywords=GSP282&event=Your
  3. Enter your Access Code received in the email
  4. Log in with your Google Cloud account
  5. You'll receive 9 initial credits → start lab "A Tour of Google Cloud Hands-on Labs"
  6. Complete the lab (min. 6 minutes, 100% score) → get 600 credits automatically

5

Start Earning Skill Badges & Points

Now that you have your 600 credits, you can start your cloud learning journey:

  • Use your credits to complete labs and skill badges
  • Each completion earns you Arcade Points
  • You'll unlock mini games and milestones during the journey
  • Complete skill badges to move up the leaderboard

Focus on completing Arcade Games & Trivia first as they have monthly deadlines.

6

Track Your Progress

Your progress will be tracked throughout the program:

  • You'll get Arcade progress updates via email
  • Your facilitator will receive reports to monitor your performance
  • Try to complete all challenges and rank high on the leaderboard
  • Track your badge completions on your Google Cloud profile
7

Swag Claiming Process

Once you meet the badge milestones, you'll be eligible for exclusive Google Cloud swag:

  1. Google Cloud team will send you an email notification to claim your swag
  2. The email will include a Swag Redemption Form (limited-time only)
  3. Fill it with your correct shipping address, mobile number, and name
  4. Submit the form before the deadline mentioned

Swag delivery can take 1-2 months after program completion. Be patient!

8

Get Professional Certification

On program completion, you'll earn valuable credentials for your career:

  • Official skill badges and certificates from Google Cloud
  • Showcase them on your LinkedIn profile and resume
  • Add to your professional portfolio to boost job prospects
